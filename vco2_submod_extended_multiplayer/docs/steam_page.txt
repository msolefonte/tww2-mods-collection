[h2]Introduction[/h2]
Some times you are just tired of farming achievements. This mod brings them to you. It is a clear cheat so use it knowing what it does. Obviously, it is not compatible with other victory conditions submods or modded faction, as it does not make sense at all.

[h2]Introduced Changes[/h2]
You start a campaign. You win. Nice.
